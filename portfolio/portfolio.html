About Me

My name is Jacob Moody. I'm studying Virtual Design and Construction at Brigham Young University - Idaho. In my time at BYU-I, I've become proficient with software including Revit, AutoCAD, Sketchup, Navisworks, Twin motion, and Lumion. My education has taught me an immense amount of information regarding construction and modeling that I was originally unfamiliar with. Even through my education I still consider myself a beginner and I feel that there is so much more I can learn. When I have free time, I love to draw portraits of my favorite characters. I also thoroughly enjoy going on beautiful hikes. Finally, I've recently become invested in learning and playing golf.
